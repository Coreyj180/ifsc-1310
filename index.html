Thoughts on Porgressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement Article

Progressive Enhancement is a common practice among web developers. It's not really a rule to follow but a great way to practice developing a website. In a nutshell it states that when developoing a website to make sure your content is accessible to all users no matter the hardware they are using. The basic functionality of the website should also be accessible to all users and HTML does a great job of it on its own without to much CSS. But also using CSS in a way that any web browser or hardware is capable of processing it no matter what.

Progressive Enhancement is important to modern web development because it reaches people and gives access to websites that they wouldn't usually have access too, due to old hardware, living in rural areas where they internet is spotty at best. It also means that you are not stuck using one browser, you have many to choose from that fit your needs. Also in a great way that it gives developers a kind template that they all use which cuts down on time.
